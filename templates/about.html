AI Disaster Resource Allocation System

The AI Disaster Resource Allocation System is a cutting-edge machine learning platform designed to revolutionize emergency response through predictive analytics.

This intelligent system analyzes multiple disaster-related parameters including population density, rainfall patterns, damage assessment, and disaster types to accurately predict and allocate emergency resources in real-time.

Key Benefit

Helps authorities and disaster management teams make data-driven decisions within minutes, potentially saving thousands of lives during critical situations.

🤖 Machine Learning

Powered by a sophisticated Random Forest Regressor with 95% accuracy in predicting resource requirements including shelters, food packets, rescue teams, and potential casualties.

Accuracy: 95% Real-time
